# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, inclusive experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of expected behavior:

- Use respectful language.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Focus on technical outcomes and collaboration.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Conduct that is inappropriate in a professional environment.

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported through the repository issue tracker or by contacting maintainers directly.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.
